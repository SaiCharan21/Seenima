Member Pricing


Basic/Non-Member

As for non-members, the prices for a flim is $1.99 for one day and $3.49 for two days and When a non-member rents a movie for a certain period, it will automatically be removed from your watchlist at that time with no additional charges.

Premium

To become a premium member, the user has to pay $7.99 monthly which includes limited two-day rentals.

Platinum

To become a premium member, the user has to pay $11.99 monthly which includes unlimited movie rentals that will include unlimited use of Sharewatch with any number of friends. Sharewatch is a feature that will allow the user to watch the movie virtually with family and friends.


Accepted Payment Methods

All payments will be done on a month to month basis with autopay turned on default.

Users will have to cancel atleast 2 days before the end of the next billing period.
