Prices

Once user create a account they will have a 7 days free trail to experience a wide selection of movies to enjoy from all genres.

To join as a member, the user has to pay a $7.99 dollars monthly subscription fee that includes unlimited rentals until your membership expires.

As for non-members, the prices for a flim is $1.99 for one day and $3.49 for two days and When a non-member rents a movie for a certain period, it will automatically be removed from your watchlist at that time with no additional charges.

As for the payment, Customers can rent movies or purchase a membership via credit card, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or alternatively, Bitcoin. While signing into their to accounts to manage their account, billing, favorite movies, and much more.
